About Me

Why did you do Ironhack?

I've always been amazed at how things work. How a toy or tool can have many singular pieces working together. I began to dissect my toys and my father's work tools to satisfy my curiosity and the "why" of how they work. I grew up to find that this is still my drive. So coming to Ironhack was a great connection to my childhood, my professional and academic experience. With UX/UI Bootcamp, I just created a point of intersection between my engineering and design background.?
